OUR MANIFESTO

After exhaustive consultation with our community, APNA GURGAON APNA BADSHAPUR TEAM has identified the SEVEN KEY FOCUS AREAS

1.Infrastructure

APNA GURGAON APNA BADSHAPUR Team believes that a massive overhaul is required in the city Key problems which persist are Flooding, Broken roads, almost nil public transport system, collapsing law and order system, piling garbage all over, unauthorized construction, etc

  • Flooding
  • Broken roads
  • Nil Public Transport
  • Piling garbage
  • unauthorized construction

2. Consumer Rights

APNA GURGAON / APNA BADSHAPUR TEAM understands that, for too long have our rights as owners been compromised for whatever reasons, causing great harm to almost all of us. We are committed to defending the rights of each property owner in our city

3. Environment & Improving Liveability

APNA Gurgaon Team believes that a community that gives back to society is blessed in return with abundance and goodwill. Together as a community we will try to build a feeling of togetherness and sense of pride towards Gurugram. We are aware of the depleting water table, lost green cover. We are committed to work towards it.

5. Education & Community Outreach

APNA Gurgaon Team strongly believes in upgradation of the government schools. Development of centers to teach spoken English and to prepare the children for competitive exams. Children who for some reason leave their studies midway find very difficult to find jobs and join mainstream. It will be our endeavor to start centers, where skills like carpentry, stitching, plumbing, etc can be taught enabling the Children to stand on their own feet. city

5. Better Legislation

APNA Gurgaon / APNA Badshapur Team knows that the Haryana Registration and Regulation of Societies Act, 2012 does not show clear direction as far as Apartment Societies are concerned due to which we all, almost on daily basis find ourselves standing in the office of District Registrar. We will work towards streamlining the HRRS Act.

6. Women safety and security

APNA GURGAON / APNA BADSHAPUR Team believes Women's safety is about ensuring that women are free from violence, harassment, and discrimination, and that they can live their lives with autonomy and financial security. Women's safety is important to us because it allows women to fulfill their potential as individuals and contributors to their communities, economies, and workplaces

7. Improved Healthcare

APNA Gurgaon / APNA Badshapur Team believes that the health and well being of our Community is of paramount importance. Our team will focus on developing Primary health Care and Better Health Education coupled with usage of information technology to Improve health of the citizens of our city city
